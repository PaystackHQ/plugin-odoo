Description

Paystack makes it easy for businesses in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya and South Africa to accept secure payments from multiple local and global payment channels. Integrate Paystack with your store today, and let your customers pay you with their choice of methods.

With Paystack for Odoo, you can accept payments via:

  • Credit/Debit Cards — Visa, Mastercard, Verve (NG, GH, KE), American Express (SA only)
  • Bank transfer (Nigeria)
  • Mobile money (Ghana)
  • Masterpass (South Africa)
  • EFT (South Africa)
  • USSD (Nigeria)
  • Visa QR (Nigeria)
  • POS (Nigeria)
  • Many more coming soon

Over 60,000 businesses of all sizes in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and South Africa rely on Paystack’s suite of products to receive payments and make payouts seamlessly. Sign up on Paystack.com/signup to get started.

Note

This plugin is meant to be used by merchants in Ghana, Kenya, Nigeria and South Africa.

Plugin Features

  • Accept payment via Mastercard, Visa, Verve, USSD, Mobile Money, Bank Transfer, EFT, Bank Accounts, GTB 737 & Visa QR.
  • Seamless integration into the Odoo checkout page. Accept payment directly on your site
  • Refunds from the Odoo order details page. Refund an order directly from the order details page
  • POS - With Odoo's Point of Sale integration, you can accept now offline payments from your customers using Paystack Terminal
